About Us

Discover our story and values

Samora Africa Safaris

Founded in 2015, Samora Africa Safaris has been dedicated to providing unforgettable safari experiences across East Africa. Our mission is to connect travelers with the stunning landscapes and diverse wildlife of Uganda and Rwanda.

With over 20 years of combined experience in the tourism industry, our team knows every corner of Uganda and Rwanda. We pride ourselves on sustainable tourism practices and supporting local communities.
