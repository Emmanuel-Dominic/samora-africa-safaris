About Us

Discover our story and values

Samora Africa Safaris

Samora Africa Safaris is a tour management company based and legally registered in Uganda with several years of expertise in crafting authentic and inclusive travel experiences across East Africa. We offer exceptional and professionally tailored safari packages to meet your travel expectations and aspirations. Our safari packages include thrilling mountain gorilla and chimpanzee encounters, captivating wildlife viewing experiences, bird-watching excursions, captivating water activities, and unique combinations of culture and nature.

At Samora Africa Safaris, we pride ourselves on organizing tours for various categories of travelers. Whether you're a couple looking for a romantic getaway, a family seeking an adventure-filled holiday, or a solo traveler eager to explore the world on your terms, we've got you covered. No matter the group size or travel preference, our team works closely with you to design an itinerary that reflects your unique needs, interests, and dreams.

Our team comprises dedicated professionals passionate about travel and committed to providing exceptional service. We practice sustainable travel to ensure that your safari adventures contribute to the continued conservation and betterment of communities, making your journey through the region more enriching and meaningful.

Why Choose Samora Africa Safaris?

Trustworthy

We rigorously apply any funds paid to us for the safari or any other necessary service. We consistently update our clients on our procurements, ensuring their confidence and reducing their concerns about their deposits.

Legitimate

We are a legitimate, reliable, and professional company, duly incorporated under the Uganda Companies Act and licensed by the Uganda Tourism Board to offer tourism services. As such, our operations are subject to regulation and oversight by the relevant statutory authorities.

Flexibility

We embrace flexibility and adaptability by customizing our services to accommodate diverse needs and preferences to ensure a seamless travel experience.

Customer Focus

We prioritize our client's wishes and aspirations by delivering exceptional, personalized service that goes beyond expectations.

Passion

Our operations are motivated by our steadfast passion and zeal for showcasing the diverse richness of Uganda and its neighboring countries to travelers from across the globe.

Integrity

To maintain integrity in our organization, we adhere to the strict guidelines established by being transparent, just, honest, and fair.

Teamwork

We value collaboration and shared responsibility. We work together seamlessly to achieve common goals and provide unwavering support to each other so that we can better serve our clients.

Sustainability

We tirelessly endeavor to reduce our negative impact on the ecosystem by adhering to sustainable tourism practices for long-term conservation of natural beauty.

Our Vision

We aim to attract travel enthusiasts from across the world to explore the fascinating beauty of Africa, particularly East Africa, by offering them exceptional travel experiences that evoke awe and appreciation for Africa's natural wonders and diverse cultural heritage.

Maureen Kaitesi
M.D & Tour Planner

Kaitesi Maureen is the Director of Samora Africa Safaris and has a deep passion for travel and a commitment to delivering the highest level of service to clients, ensuring their safari experiences are extraordinary and unforgettable. Her deep appreciation for nature and wildlife began to take shape during the numerous field trips organized by her secondary school, which ventured into various regions across the country. Born just a few kilometers from Kampala city and educated in schools within the urban area, she always looked forward to these excursions with eager anticipation. These trips enabled her to witness breathtaking landscapes, encounter diverse wildlife, and connect with new people, fueling her curiosity and love for exploration.

After graduating from university and securing employment, she dedicated most of her weekends to traveling across Uganda, immersing herself in the country's natural wonders. This early exposure to the beauty of nature not only deepened her passion for travel but also inspired her to establish Samora Africa Safaris. Through this venture, she aims to share the splendor of Uganda with the world, inviting others to experience its captivating landscapes and rich biodiversity.

In addition to her passion for travel and professional ethics, Kaitesi Maureen is trustworthy, dependable, and detail-oriented. She guarantees a smooth and hassle-free booking process by offering clients accurate information and promptly addressing all inquiries. Her dedication to perfection means that every aspect of the safari is taken into account from the initial inquiry stage up to the conclusion of the safari.

Akello Jessica
Reservations Officer

Akello Jessica is the Reservations Officer at Samora Africa Safaris, responsible for ensuring that all records of reservations, bookings, payments, clients' preferences, and flight details are accurate and well documented. Her meticulous attention to detail and organizational skills ensure that every client's travel arrangements are handled with precision and care.

Jessica plays a vital role in coordinating between clients and service providers, ensuring seamless communication throughout the booking process. She takes pride in providing timely responses to inquiries and confirmations, giving clients peace of mind as they prepare for their safari adventures.

Her dedication to maintaining accurate and up-to-date records helps the team deliver a smooth and hassle-free experience for every traveler, from the initial booking to the final day of the safari.

Norman Mugabo
Lead Tour Guide

Norman Mugabo is the Lead Tour Guide at Samora Africa Safaris, bringing over 8 years of experience leading successful safaris across Rwanda and Uganda. His deep knowledge of East Africa's breathtaking landscapes, diverse wildlife, and rich cultural heritage makes every safari an enriching and memorable experience.

Norman's expertise spans gorilla trekking in the misty forests of Bwindi and Volcanoes National Park, wildlife game drives across the savannah plains, and cultural community visits that offer authentic African experiences. His ability to spot wildlife and share insightful commentary about the ecosystems and animal behavior sets him apart as a guide.

Known for his friendly demeanor, professionalism, and commitment to client safety, Norman ensures that every traveler feels comfortable and well-cared for throughout their journey. His passion for showcasing the natural beauty of East Africa is evident in every safari he leads.

Nakafeero Veronica
Tour Guide / Woman Birder

Nakafeero Veronica is a highly experienced Tour Guide and one of the best women birders in Uganda. With over 8 years of guiding tourists throughout Uganda, Rwanda, Kenya, and Tanzania, she brings a wealth of knowledge and passion to every safari experience.

Veronica's exceptional birding skills make her the ideal guide for bird-watching enthusiasts visiting Uganda's renowned birding spots, from the forests of Kibale and Bwindi to the savannah parks and wetlands. She has an extraordinary ability to identify bird species by sight and sound, and her enthusiasm for ornithology is infectious.

Beyond birding, Veronica is a versatile guide capable of leading wildlife safaris, gorilla treks, and cultural tours. Her warm personality, deep connection to the natural environment, and dedication to providing outstanding service ensure that every traveler has an unforgettable experience in East Africa.
